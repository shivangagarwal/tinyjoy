## 3. Description (300 words)

TinyJoy is a collection of 19 free, browser-based games designed for quick, satisfying play. Open the site, pick a game, start playing in seconds. No account. No download. No paywall.

**The games span five categories:**

- **Puzzle** — 2048, Sudoku, Minesweeper, Solitaire, Connect Four
- **Arcade** — Snake, Brick Breaker, Flappy Jump, Whack-a-Mole
- **Memory & Pattern** — Memory Flip, Pattern Echo
- **Word** — Word Scramble, Word Guess, Hangman
- **Skill & Speed** — Typing Speed, Reaction Time, Number Rush, Color Match, Tic-Tac-Toe

Each game is small, focused, and fast to load — built for the moments between meetings, during a commute, or when you just need to decompress without committing to a full gaming session.

TinyJoy is web-first and mobile-friendly. Everything runs in your browser with zero friction. There's no lobby, no level-gating, no energy system, no ads interrupting gameplay.

We built TinyJoy because the casual gaming space is cluttered with apps demanding installs, accounts, and attention. We wanted the opposite: a calm corner of the internet where you can play something good without any overhead.

The site is live at [tinyjoy.app](https://tinyjoy.app). Everything is free. We're indie — just a small team shipping games we'd want to play ourselves.

## 7. Launch Day Recommendation

**Best day:** Tuesday or Wednesday

**Best time:** 12:01 AM PT (Pacific Time) — Product Hunt resets at midnight PT, and early votes in the first 2-3 hours heavily determine daily ranking. Posting at 12:01 AM PT maximizes your window.

Avoid Monday (lower traffic) and Friday (weekend dropoff). Tuesday and Wednesday consistently see the highest Product Hunt engagement.

## 8. Upvote Strategy

### Before launch
- [ ] Prepare a short personal list of 20–30 genuine supporters (friends, past colleagues, indie dev communities) to notify the moment the post goes live
- [ ] Draft a personal DM template to send on launch day (see below)
- [ ] Post a "launching tomorrow on Product Hunt" teaser on Twitter/X the day before
- [ ] Notify any Discord communities (indie hackers, web game dev servers) 24h before

### Launch day
- [ ] Post at 12:01 AM PT exactly
- [ ] Share the PH link immediately on Twitter/X: "We're live on Product Hunt today! Would love your support: [link]"
- [ ] Post in relevant subreddits: r/webgames, r/indiegaming, r/producthunt — genuine "launched today" style post
- [ ] Post in IndieHackers — they support PH launches actively
- [ ] Send personal DMs to the supporter list immediately (not blast — personal messages perform far better)
- [ ] Respond to every comment on PH within 30 minutes — early engagement signals recency to PH algorithm

### Personal DM template
```
Hey [Name]! I launched TinyJoy on Product Hunt today — it's a collection of 19 free browser games (no download, no signup). If you have 30 seconds, a vote would mean a lot: [PH link]. Thanks!
```

### What NOT to do
- Do not ask for upvotes in communities that ban it (some subreddits will remove your post)
- Do not use upvote rings or paid services — PH detects and penalizes them
- Do not post the link in Slack groups or Discord and ask members to "upvote" — frame as "check it out" instead
